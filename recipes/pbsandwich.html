Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

The American classic with lots of options like crunchy peanut Butter to strawberry jelly.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of your favorite bread
  • Your choice of jelly or jam

Steps to create a delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Take the 2 slices of bread and set them side by side
  2. Take a knife and spread peanut butter on one of the slices
  3. Take another knife and spread the jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Combine those 2 slices together to create a peanut butter and jelly center
