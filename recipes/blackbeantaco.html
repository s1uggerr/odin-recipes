Plant-Based Black Bean Taco

The black bean taco is an excellent dish for those who love tacos and are avoiding meat. To cook this meal you will need a dutch oven and the ingredients. It should take no longer than an hour to make and you will love it.

ingredients

    • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
      1 medium onion, chopped
      1 medium green bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
      2 stalks celery, chopped
      2 medium carrots, chopped
      2 medium jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
      4 cloves garlic, chopped
      other important ingredients

    • Steps

    • Heat the oven
      Stir the ingredients and heat for 30 minutes
